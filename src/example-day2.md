# Day 2 — 25 分鐘口說練習

## 今日主題：氣候變遷與能源轉型

**【HF】高頻內容詞（12 個）**：reduce, increase, cause, lead, affect, require, achieve, evidence, policy, solution, risk, cost

**【BUNDLE】高頻詞組（3 個）**：

- The evidence suggests that…
- One of the main challenges is…
- This leads to the conclusion that…

---

## 0–3 分鐘：校準句

**句子**：In my view, the main POINT is simple, but the details matter.

**標註**：In my VIEW / the main POINT is SIMple / but the deTAILS MATter.

**要點**：

- 功能詞弱讀：in /ɪn/, my /maɪ→mɪ/, the /ðə/, is /ɪz→z/, but /bət/
- 句尾 MATter 下降終止，明確收束
- but 前後形成邊界（前段半終止、後段終止）

**三遍法**：

1. 第 1 遍：慢速，只求重音位置正確
2. 第 2 遍：正常語速，加上自然節奏
3. 第 3 遍：錄音一次

## 3–9 分鐘：音段對立（2 句）

### 句 1（/iː/–/ɪ/ 對立）

**句子**：Rising sea levels will 【HF】LEAD to increased flooding, which could 【HF】AFFECT millions living near the coast.

**標註**：Rising SEA levels / will LEAD to increased FLOODing / which could afFECT MILlions / living near the COAST.

**操作化目標**：

- sea /siː/ 長音 vs. living /lɪvɪŋ/ 短音
- millions /ˈmɪljənz/ 的 /ɪ/ 不可拉長
- will、to、could、the 弱讀
- LEAD_to、near_the 連結
- COAST 句尾核重音並下降終止

### 句 2（/θ/–/ð/ 對立）

**句子**：【BUNDLE】The 【HF】EVIDENCE suggests that the Earth's temperature has risen by more than one degree Celsius since the nineteenth century.

**標註**：The EVidence sugGESTS / that the Earth's TEMperature / has risen by MORE than one degree / since the nineteenth CENtury.

**操作化目標**：

- The /ðə/ 濁音 vs. Earth's /θ/ 清音 — 舌位相同但送氣不同
- nineteenth /ˌnaɪnˈtiːnθ/ 結尾的 /nθ/ 需清楚
- that_the 連結，that 弱讀 /ðət/
- CENtury 句尾下降終止

## 9–15 分鐘：超音段焦點與對比（2 句）

### 句 3（數據對比）

**句子**：Global emissions need to 【HF】REDUCE by forty-five percent by 2030, not twenty percent.

**讀法 A**（強調 45% 這個數字）：

- 標註：Global eMISsions / need to reDUCE by FORTY-five percent / by twenty THIRty / not twenty percent.
- 核重音在 FORTY-five，末端下降終止

**讀法 B**（強調「不是 20%」的否定）：

- 標註：Global emissions need to reduce by forty-five percent / by 2030 / NOT TWENTY percent.
- 核重音轉移到 TWENTY，NOT 前語調略抬形成對比

### 句 4（政策立場＋轉折）

**句子**：Nuclear energy 【HF】PROVIDES low-carbon power, but the 【HF】RISK of accidents and waste disposal remains a serious concern.

**讀法 A**（轉折邊界清楚）：

- 標註：Nuclear ENergy / proVIDES low-carbon POWer / but the RISK of ACcidents / and waste disPOsal / remains a serious conCERN.
- but 前半終止、後段重新起調；concern 下降終止

**讀法 B**（焦點在風險）：

- 標註：Nuclear energy provides low-carbon power / but the RISK / of accidents and WASTE disPOsal / remains a SERIOUS conCERN.
- 核重音落在 RISK 與 SERIOUS，強調擔憂

## 15–20 分鐘：連續語流＋語篇模板（2 句）

### 句 5（縮約＋因果論述）

**句子**：If we'd invested in renewable energy earlier, we wouldn't be facing such high 【HF】COSTS now — but we didn't, and that's the 【HF】POLICY failure.

**標註**：If we'd inVESted / in reNEWable ENergy EARlier / we wouldn't be FAcing / such high COSTS now / but we DIDN'T / and that's the POlicy FAILure.

**連續語流目標**：

- we'd /wiːd/（we had 縮約）
- wouldn't /ˈwʊdnt/（would not 縮約）
- that's /ðæts/（that is 縮約）
- invested_in 連結
- FAILure 句尾核重音並下降終止

### 句 6（BUNDLE＋數據引用）

**句子**：【BUNDLE】One of the main 【HF】CHALLENGES is that switching to electric vehicles 【HF】REQUIRES massive infrastructure investment, estimated at over two hundred billion dollars in the US alone.

**標註**：One of the main CHALlenges is / that SWITCHing to eLECtric VEhicles / reQUIRES massive infraSTRUCture inVESTment / estimated at over two hundred BILlion DOLlars / in the US aLONE.

**連續語流目標**：

- One_of 連結
- that 弱讀 /ðət/
- at_over 連結
- aLONE 句尾核重音並下降終止

## 20–25 分鐘：語篇組織＋結論收束（2 句）

### 句 7（although/however＋讓步轉折）

**句子**：Although carbon capture technology shows promise, it currently 【HF】COSTS too much to deploy at scale; however, continued research could 【HF】LEAD to breakthroughs.

**標註**：AlTHOUGH / carbon CAPture techNOLogy / shows PROMise / it currently COSTS too much / to dePLOY at SCALE / howEVer / continued reSEARCH / could LEAD to BREAKthroughs.

**要點**：

- Although 與 however 必須獨立成短語塊（前後各有邊界停頓 150–250 ms）
- SCALE 為前段核重音
- BREAKthroughs 句尾下降終止

### 句 8（therefore＋結論）

**句子**：【BUNDLE】This 【HF】LEADS to the conclusion that without immediate action to 【HF】REDUCE emissions, we will fail to 【HF】ACHIEVE the Paris Agreement targets — and the consequences will be irreversible.

**標註**：This LEADS to the conCLUsion / that without immEdiate ACtion / to reDUCE eMISsions / we will FAIL / to aCHIEVE the Paris aGREEment TARgets / and the CONsequences / will be irreVERsible.

**要點**：

- This leads to the conclusion 作為 BUNDLE 需流暢讀出
- that 弱讀
- to 弱讀 /tə/
- irreVERsible 句尾核重音並下降終止，傳達嚴肅警告語氣
